Volunteering at the bike shop

Volunteers make our shop run! Want to give back to the community, make cycling more accessible, and learn all about bikes? Come join a team of friendly people with a passion for all sorts of bikes! A desire to learn and help others is all you need. No previous bike repair experience is required.

Volunteer responsibilities

  • welcome patrons and help them to find bikes and parts
  • repair bikes and help with DIY repairs
  • fix up bikes and parts to sell
  • organize, tidy and improve the shop
  • undertake special projects

Volunteer responsibilities

  • a free Bike Edmonton membership
  • free use of the workshop, and eligibility for a shop key 
  • discounts on parts and bikes
  • mechanical training
  • opportunities for paid mechanic and event work
  • volunteer events and group bike rides!

Volunteer responsibilities

  • you must be 16 or over to volunteer
  • minimum time commitment of 3-4 hours every two weeks
  • follow all shop rules
  • be willing to help out with all duties needed to keep the shop running

Starting out

Step 1

Visit our shop during open hours

Step 2

Fill out the Volunteer Application form

Step 3

Attend an information session

Step 4

Sign up for your first shift and start volunteering!
